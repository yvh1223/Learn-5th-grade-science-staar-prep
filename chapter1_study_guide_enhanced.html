🔬 The Scientific Method - 6 Steps

Remember: "Please Question High Ideas Before Concluding"
1. Problem/Question
2. Questioning/Research
3. Hypothesis
4. Investigation/Experiment
5. Behavior/Observation
6. Conclusion
Hypothesis must follow "If... then... because..." format!
Example: "If I add fertilizer to plants, then they will grow taller because fertilizer provides nutrients."
Variable
Something that can change in an experiment
Control Group
The group that doesn't receive the treatment (for comparison)
Data
Information collected during an experiment

📏 Scientific Tools & Measurement

"RBTGS" = "Really Big Things Go Slowly"
📏 Ruler - measures length (cm, m)
⚖️ Balance - measures mass (g, kg)
🌡️ Thermometer - measures temperature (°C)
🥤 Graduated cylinder - measures liquid volume (mL)
⏱️ Stopwatch - measures time (s, min)
When reading graduated cylinders, always read from the BOTTOM of the meniscus (curved water surface) at eye level!

Common STAAR Question Types:

  • Which tool measures mass? → Balance
  • Which tool measures liquid volume? → Graduated cylinder
  • Reading measurements from diagrams
  • Choosing appropriate units (g for mass, mL for volume)

👁️ Observations vs. Inferences

Qualitative = Qualities (no numbers): red, smooth, loud
Quantitative = Quantities (has numbers): 15 cm, 25°C, 50 mL
Observation Inference
What you SEE, HEAR, SMELL, TASTE, FEEL Conclusion or explanation based on observations
"The ice cubes disappeared" "The ice melted because it was warm"
"The plant leaves are yellow" "The plant needs water"
Look for words like "concludes," "thinks," "believes," or "because" for inferences!

📝 Quick Practice Questions

Q1: What is the FIRST step in the scientific method?
Answer: Ask a question or identify the problem
Q2: A student observes: "The water is bubbling and the temperature is 100°C." Which part is quantitative?
Answer: "100°C" (has numbers/measurement)
Q3: Which tool would you use to find the mass of a rock?
Answer: Balance or scale
Q4: "If I water plants daily, then they will grow faster because water helps plants make food." This is an example of a:
Answer: Hypothesis (follows If...then...because format)

🎯 STAAR Test-Taking Strategies

Time Management: Don't spend more than 2 minutes per question. Skip hard ones and come back.
Process of Elimination: Cross out obviously wrong answers first, then choose the best remaining option.
Key Words: Look for "FIRST step," "BEST tool," "most likely," "based on the data."
