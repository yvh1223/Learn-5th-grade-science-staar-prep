💡 Study Tips for Success

📅 Daily Schedule

Study 1 chapter every 3-4 days. Take breaks and review previous chapters!

🧪 Hands-On Learning

Try the experiments! Science is best learned by doing and exploring.

❓ Ask Questions

Write down questions as you learn. Research answers or ask adults for help!

📝 Take Notes

Keep a science journal. Drawing diagrams helps you remember better!
