STAAR & MAP Success Guide

‚è∞ Time Management Strategies

STAAR Science: 32 questions in 4 hours = ~7 minutes per question maximum
  • 2-Minute Rule: Don't spend more than 2 minutes on any single question
  • Mark & Move: Circle difficult questions and return to them
  • Easy First: Answer all questions you know quickly, then tackle harder ones
  • Save 15 Minutes: Reserve time at the end to review marked questions

üìù Answer Elimination Techniques

Remember: Even if you don't know the answer, you can often eliminate 2-3 wrong choices!
  1. Cross Out Obviously Wrong: Look for answers that don't make sense
  2. Check Units: Does the answer have the right measurement units?
  3. Extreme Answers: Very large or very small numbers are often wrong
  4. Keywords Matter: "ALWAYS" and "NEVER" answers are usually incorrect

üî¨ Science-Specific Tips

  • Scientific Method: Always start with "Ask a Question"
  • Tools: Match tool to what it measures
  • Observations: Use your 5 senses only
  • Hypothesis: Look for "If...then...because" format
  • Data Tables: Read labels and units carefully

üìä Graph & Data Questions

  • Title First: Always read the graph title
  • Check Axes: What do X and Y represent?
  • Find Scale: What does each mark mean?
  • Look for Trends: Going up, down, or staying same?
  • Compare Data: Which is highest/lowest?

üß† Test Day Mental Strategies

Before the Test:

  • Get good sleep (8+ hours)
  • Eat a healthy breakfast
  • Review key concepts for 15 minutes
  • Stay positive and confident

During the Test:

  • Read questions carefully
  • Take deep breaths if stressed
  • Trust your first instinct
  • Don't second-guess too much
